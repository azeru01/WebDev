Hobbies

During the week I am busy with work and school. However, I take Saturday off from work and school related work. I wake up around 6:00 am in the morning sometimes earlier, to do my weekly hike. There is a group/ organization called 510 Hiker in Oakland, CA. that hikes every Saturday morning for about 8 to 12 miles. I am part of the group and do my hiking in different parts of the Bay Area for about three hours on average. That is one of my hobbies that I take seriously and consider to be part of my self-care routine. It is also my time to disconnect from the city life that is always on the run and connect with nature. I find nature’s adventure and exploration of different trials rejuvenating my soul. It is where I detox and therapeutic from the week’s workload.

Sometimes, I hike using Bay Area Hiker guidelines and past trials and information. It is a website that has a detailed information on hikes and trails.
